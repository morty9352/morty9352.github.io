Task Tabata

20 seconds of pull-ups
10 seconds of rest
20 seconds of push-ups
10 seconds of rest
20 seconds of situps
10 seconds of rest
20 seconds of squats
10 seconds of rest
After the round of squats, begin again with the pull-ups. Keep a running total of reps and continue
working until 300 reps are completed.
